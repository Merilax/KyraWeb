Kyra! Industries

A newfound Break Arts company looking to pioneer the field of arts. We swear by our artistic values and specialise in hand-crafting modules and complex machinery meant for events, showrooms, and other high-profile displays, without directly participating in races. Additionally, Kyra! produces naval hull blueprints, which are sent over to Monolith for further development and possible use in an ever improving fleet.
You may visit our expositions here at Ragachevo, aimed to attract curious visitors and investors alike, in an unique environment isolated from the racing scene.

Furthermore, we want to support artists, no matter what level of expertise, and provide them with their own creative environment and our common knowledge of the field. Kyra! Industries is an organisation, established in Ragachevo, Russia, comprised of two divisions: Monolith, a worldwide naval industry working in cargo transportation, and a Break Arts company that shares the organisation's name. This website is dedicated to the former.
Access to the island is available mainly through Monolith transportation routes for commercial reasons, or through a nearby airport for visitors.

Monolith Nauticals

A naval industry that excels at transporting all kind of precious cargo within and through the polar region, including most of the world for a substantial increase in price. With icebergs and outer sections of the polar casket being little to no issue, we pride ourselves in rapid deliveries, setting sailing routes only we are able to cross.

Massive Monolith ships can be commonly found transporting Kyra! Industries BALC mechs to showcasing events, bringing the spectacle closer to the people.
